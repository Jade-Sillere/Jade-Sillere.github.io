National Elections Database

The National Elections Database is intended to provide a centralized database of national election results since World War II with the aim of facilitating electoral research. Consisting of 3,985 national elections, including 1,023 presidential elections and 2,962 parliamentary elections, it includes all presidential and parliamentary elections held around the world between 1946 and 2018.

Datasets available

1. Parliamentary Elections

  • Summary: Election results for all parliamentary elections from 1946 to 2018.
  • Election coverage: 2,962 parliamentary elections
  • Key information: Seat number and shares for all parties along with information on the incumbent party, margin of victory of the opposition over the incumbent party, and whether there was an electoral turnover.
  • Formats: STATA

2. Presidential Elections

  • Summary: Election results for all presidential elections from 1946 to 2018.
  • Election coverage: 1,023 presidential elections
  • Key information: Vote number and shares for all candidates along with information on the incumbent candidate, margin of victory of the challenger over the incumbent candidate, and whether there was an electoral turnover.
  • Formats: STATA
